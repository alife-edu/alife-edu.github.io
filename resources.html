Community Resources

Teaching Artificial Life — a resource collection

A curated set of references, tools, courses, and interactive explorables for anyone teaching or learning about Artificial Life. This page is a living collection — contributions welcome.

01

Organizations & reference

Wiki
ALife Wiki
The community-maintained wiki for Artificial Life — covering key concepts, historical context, research areas, and links to seminal papers. A good starting point for anyone new to the field.
Visit wiki ↗
Code
ISAL GitHub
This repository is for lecture slides, exercises, assessments, exam questions for teachers and students of Artificial Life.
View repositories ↗
Newsletter
ALife Newsletter
A community newsletter covering recent developments, research highlights, and events in the Artificial Life community.
Read newsletter ↗
Society
ISAL — International Society for Artificial Life
The primary professional society for Artificial Life researchers. ISAL organizes the annual ALIFE conference, publishes the Artificial Life journal, and supports the global ALife research community.
Visit ISAL ↗
Conference
ALIFE 2026
The next annual ALIFE conference — August 17–21, 2026 in Waterloo, Canada. Held jointly with the AMMCS Congress at Wilfrid Laurier University and the University of Waterloo.
Visit 2026.alife.org ↗
