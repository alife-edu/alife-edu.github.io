About the workshop

1 / 2

Each two-day workshop explores fundamental questions about teaching Artificial Life — Day 1 focuses on the "Why?" and Day 2 on the "How?"

Day 1

"Why teach ALife?"

Align on shared goals and learning outcomes for ALife education. Small group discussions surface the challenges and opportunities ALife presents as a teaching domain. Features curated presentations and lightning talks showcasing ALife education success stories.

Day 2

"How to teach ALife?"

Building on Day 1 goals, shift to practical strategies. Small group feedback exchanges let you workshop your own ideas and teaching artifacts with peers. Experienced educators lead introductory tutorials on effective pedagogical practices.
